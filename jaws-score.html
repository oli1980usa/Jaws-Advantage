We use cookies and local storage to make this site work and to understand how it's used. No ads. No selling your data.

Essential Always On

  • Dark/light mode preference — saved locally on your device
  • Daily chat question limit — saved locally so the chatbot tracks your daily usage
  • Article ratings — saved locally so the site remembers if you've already rated an article
  • Chat session history — stored temporarily in your browser only, never on our servers

Analytics With Your Consent

  • Google Analytics — anonymous page views and usage patterns. No personally identifiable information.
  • Quiz score events — your score tier (e.g. Tiger Shark) sent to Google Analytics anonymously. Exact score not stored by us.
  • Article rating counts — aggregate vote counts stored on our servers with no personal information attached.

We Don't

  • Sell your data
  • Use advertising cookies
  • Collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • Track you across other websites
  • Store your chat conversations on our servers
