Most people work hard.

Even fewer understand the game.

Corporate ladders aren't climbed with effort alone. They're climbed with access, perception, and strategic relationships. The JAWS Advantage is the playbook nobody hands you on your first day — because most people in the room don't want you to have it.

The Playbook

Four Pillars of Career Dominance

01

Engineer Your Presence

Visibility is not vanity. Networking is not luck — it's architecture. The right people, the right rooms, the right moment. Being known by the people who matter before you need them is the single biggest lever in your career.

Coming Soon
02

Be Courageous and Curious

Make the call. Own it. Nobody respects a leader who delegates decisions upwards. Stay curious enough to ask better questions than everyone else — and decisive enough to act on the answers, even with imperfect information.

Coming Soon
03

Lead With and Through Others

You cannot do it alone. The shift from individual contributor to leader happens when you stop asking what you can deliver and start asking what your team can deliver. Empower them. Their success is your success.

Read the Playbook
04

Manage and Influence Upwards

The half of the job nobody talks about. Your relationship with the people above you determines your visibility, your resources and your trajectory. Most people only manage downward. The ones who move fast manage in every direction.
